About Me

Hi there! My name is Yoseph. I'm 20 years old from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I am currently a sophomore at Washington and Lee University, in the class of 2022, majoring in Computer Science. Outside of the classroom, I spend most of my time working on various personal projects as well as playing a management role in the school's African Society; my favorite hobbies include basketball, listening to music, and spending time with friends and family. I am constantly looking for opportunities to develop my skills as a future software engineer and apply the knowledge I have gained. I am excited to meet new people, embark on new adventures and develop my skills as a software engineer.
